Data Privacy & Security Protocols

Sentinel AI is built with a "Security First" architecture. We understand the sensitive nature of forensic data and adhere to strict protocols to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

End-to-End Isolation AES-256 Standards Zero-Knowledge Access

1. Data Ownership & Control

Your agency retains full ownership of all data uploaded to the system. Sentinel AI acts solely as a data processor. We do not claim rights to any evidence, reports, or logs generated within the platform.

2. Evidence Isolation

We utilize a compartmentalized database architecture. Each investigation case functions as an independent, encrypted container. This ensures that data from one case cannot be accessed, indexed, or influenced by another, eliminating the risk of evidence contamination.

3. Data Protection Measures

  • Encryption at Rest: All stored media and generated reports are encrypted on the server.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission occurs over secure HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) channels.
  • Access Control: Strict authentication protocols ensure only authorized personnel can access case files.
  • Automatic Purging: Temporary analysis files used by our AI processors are deleted immediately after report generation.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We maintain a strict No-Sharing Policy. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties. External AI processing partners (e.g., Google Cloud) are utilized strictly for computational analysis under enterprise confidentiality agreements, with no data retention permissions.
