Operational Capabilities

A comprehensive suite of forensic tools designed to accelerate investigations and reduce manual workload for law enforcement.

Automated Evidence Analysis

Eliminate hours of manual video review. Our system automatically scans footage to identify key events, people, and anomalies, producing a summary report in seconds.

📝

Instant Report Generation

Convert visual data into professional text reports immediately. The system logs timestamps, descriptions, and critical actions for official case files.

Smart Case Interrogation

Interact with your evidence naturally. Ask questions like "What color was the getaway vehicle?" or "Describe the suspect's clothing" to retrieve facts instantly.

Adaptive Intelligence

The system learns from officer input. If you correct a detail (e.g., updating a license plate number), the AI permanently updates its understanding of the case.

🛡️

Strict Case Isolation

Maintain absolute integrity. Evidence and data from one investigation are cryptographically isolated, preventing any cross-contamination between cases.

A secure, unified digital locker for all case media. Upload, organize, and retrieve video evidence from a single dashboard accessible only to authorized personnel.
